<br>
<i>Invisible through Bach</i> is an immersive sound-visual art work composed between 2018 and 2019, for an architecture of resonant/reflective motorized metal plates, cello, live electronics and live visual art.

<br><br> <i>Invisible through Bach</i>, presented for <a href="http://www.oggimusica.ch/" target="_blank">OGGIMUSICA 2018-2019</a>, in collaboration with <a href="http://www.luganomusica.ch/" target="_blank">LuganoMusica</a> at the theatre <a href="https://www.luganolac.ch/" target="_blank"> LAC</a> of Lugano (CH), on 19 March 2019, brings together all the most recent developments of my artistic and technical researches. As a result of a collaboration with cellist Giacomo Cardelli and visual artist Rajan Craveri, it was supported by the <a href="http://delz.ch/en/index.htm" target="_blank">Christop Delz Foundation</a> of Basel (CH).
